Login by AO(1441180), then click "TICKET CHECKER" Menu.




You will see below picture.
It includes 4 functions, checking ticket, comparing invoice, verifying ticket and rejecting ticket.




for example CNS1510145.
AO Staff uses scanner to scan the ticket bar code. then it will auto-search from database and pop-up ticket information.




And then, AO Staff uses scanner to scan the invoice QR Code to verify the invoice correctness.
The QR information is like "01,04,3100162130,44557616,132.67,20170525,72421672501935626208,3C4C,"
You can copy "01,04,3100162130,44557616,132.67,20170525,72421672501935626208,3C4C," to "Invoice Code" textbox as correct invoice example.
You can copy "01,04,1200164320,02388138,224.27,20170430,72421672501935626208,3C4C," to "Invoice Code" textbox as incorrect invoice example.




After verifying the invoice, you can click "Verify" button to confirm this ticket and its invoices are correct and go on next payment process.
For "Reject" function, it doesn't support in demo.
